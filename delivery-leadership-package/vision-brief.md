## Who is the customer?
The customer is someone looking for a quick, no-commitment, insurance rate that can be pulled from their mobile device. They want to get the quote and insurance without signing up for an account and filling out loads of "extra" information. Most of the customers will be first-time home owners or renters who have been told "you need insurance".

## What pain does Evergreen Quote remove?
Allows the customer to get a real, quick, quote from their mobile device without signing up for an account.

## What does "good" look like at end of the week?
- A single responsive site is avaliable that loads both on mobile and desktop without horizonal scolling
- The site is able to calculate monthly rates for auto, home, and renters that can be trusted
- The "Evergreen" branding is recognizable 
- Proper coding practices are followed - pull requests, final code on 'main', etc.

## What are we explicitly NOT doing this week?
- No real rate engine or actuarial pricing — the calculator is a placeholder.
- No customer accounts, saved quotes, or email capture.
- No payment, checkout, or policy purchase.
- No back-end or database — this is a front-end-only page.

## How will we know if it worked?
- If the whole page loads and is themed without a horizonal scroll bar at ~375px wide
- The form allows for minimal input from the customer and provides a belivable quote
- All features are commited in 'main' branch of the code
